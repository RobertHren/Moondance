Speedy

Born in Belgrade in 1989. After a few years of organizing parties, he has become synonymous with Techno in Serbia as one of the credit for bringing the techno scene in the country again come to life. Since 2010. he has been the owner of the record label Lucky Light, which is distributed over the Manual Musica from Netherlands. Lucky Light is a platform for publishing music artists such as Cristian Varela, Marco Bailey, Marko Nastic, Tom Hades, Mladen Tomic, Dejan Milicevic etc. and it is supported by several famous artists in the musical sphere: Carl Cox, Mauro Picotto, Richie Hawtin, Dubfire, Cristian Varela, Marco Bailey and so on.

Same year he began a series of parties named Lucky Records Showcase where guests were Dave Clarke, Adam Beyer, Cristian Varela, Marco Bailey, Ben Sims, Mauro Picotto, Marco Nastic, Dejan Milicevic, Peter Dundov, Mladen Tomic ... At that time, Speedy also began the project called Lucky Records on tour, which included doing a big parties in the region (in Slovenia, Croatia, Bosnia and Macedonia) with the intention to present the label to the audience.

Speedy has played in all the big clubs in the region (Boogaloo, Sector 909, Jungle, Extreme, Quo Vadis, Amnesia, The Code, The Tube). The culmination of a 2011th was his performance at the biggest festival in Eastern Europe - EXIT Festival.

During the last few years Speedy has been working hard in the studio, both for his own label Lucky Light, and for labels such as Pornographic Recordings, Night Light, Vezotonika and many others. His single Wet Nurse, which is signed for Pornographic Recordings, has been the greatest success so far. Also, this single was positively rated by almost all Techno music industry today.

Since 2013. Speedy has been the family member of the Madrid Phrenetic Agency , whose general manager is Luis Varela.

Speedy consider famous Serbian DJ and producer Dejan Milicevic to be his role model and attributes to him much of the credit for everything!
