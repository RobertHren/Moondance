Moondance Festival

History

Moondance Festival is Electronic music festival launched 2013. in stunning middle age fortress Kamerlengo in one of the most beautifull world's city islands - Trogir.

It's an exclusive event for true electronic music lovers representing some of the most significant world's as well as Croatia's electronic music DJ's and live act.

Started as one night and one stage festival, Moondance expands in 2014. on two nights and two stages with official warm up and after parties.

Our goal is to offer unique music experience combining beautiful historical location, great artists and fantastic production in the middle of the summer at one of the hottest destination in Croatia.
